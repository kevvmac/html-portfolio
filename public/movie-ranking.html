Best Movie Selections for Kev

The Boy and the Heron

Slow burn, studio ghibli animation. Cathartic ending of a boy's journey through loss and grief.

Dune 2

Epic cinema. Paul and Chani's chemistry is great. Scene of Mua'dib rise to power is chilling.

The Secret Life of Walter Mitty

So artistic. The shots of Iceland with the skateboarding scene are breathtaking.
